About Meri Panchayat

Empowering Rural India through Digital Governance

The Meri Panchayat initiative is a flagship program by the Ministry of Panchayati Raj, Government of India, aimed at bringing transparency, accountability, and efficiency to rural governance.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and local administration by providing a unified platform for reporting issues, tracking public works, and accessing important notices. By leveraging technology, we ensure that every voice in the village is heard and every grievance is addressed in a time-bound manner.

Vision

To create a digitally empowered rural society where governance is accessible to the last mile.

Mission

To implement e-Governance solutions that simplify administrative processes and enhance citizen engagement.
