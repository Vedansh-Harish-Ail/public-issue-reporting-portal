About Meri Panchayat

The m-Governance Platform of Ministry of Panchayati Raj, Government of India

Meri Panchayat is an integrated m-governance platform designed to empower rural India. It facilitates transparency, accountability, and active citizen participation in local self-governance.

The platform enables citizens to report civic issues directly to their Gram Panchayat, track resolution status, and stay informed about development activities. It envisions a Digital Gram Swaraj where technology bridges the gap between administration and the people.
